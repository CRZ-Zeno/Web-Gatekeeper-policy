Effective Date: October 6th, 2025.

Web Gatekeeper and its companion extension Proctor do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data outside the user’s device.

All data, including browsing time, website limits, and usage statistics, is stored locally using Chrome’s storage.local API.

The extensions do not connect to any external servers, and no data is shared with third parties.

Permissions such as tabs, idle, and storage are used strictly for:
– tracking active website time;
– pausing time counting when the browser is inactive;
– saving user preferences locally.

By installing this extension, you acknowledge that no personal information leaves your browser.
